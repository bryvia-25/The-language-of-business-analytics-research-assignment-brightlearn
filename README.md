# Business Analytics Terminology Assignment

## Overview

This project is a collection of important terms used in Business Analytics. It explains key concepts, data terms, and marketing metrics in a simple and easy-to-understand way.

The goal of this assignment is to build a strong understanding of how businesses use data to make decisions.

## Objectives

* Understand basic Business Analytics concepts
* Learn important data and reporting terms
* Understand customer and sales metrics (KPIs)
* Learn how marketing performance is measured

## What’s Included

This assignment covers 38 terms divided into 4 sections:

1. **Foundational Concepts**
   Basic ideas like Business Analytics, Big Data, and Predictive Analytics

2. **Data, Measurement & Reporting**
   Terms like Data Warehouse, KPI, Dashboard, and Data Visualization

3. **Customer & Sales KPIs**
   Metrics like Conversion Rate, CAC, CLV, and Churn Rate

4. **Marketing & Campaign Analytics**
   Concepts like ROI, CTR, A/B Testing, and Marketing Attribution

## Structure

Each term includes:

* A short definition (in simple words)
* A real-world example
* A reference source

##  Tools & Sources Used

* Online research (business and analytics websites)
* Academic books and learning platforms
* Data and marketing resources

## Why This Matters
Business Analytics helps companies:

* Make better decisions
* Understand customers
* Improve performance
* Increase profits
